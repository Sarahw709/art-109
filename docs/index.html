My name is Sarah Wheeler, and I am a fourth-year transfer student at San Jose State University. I transferred from Las Positas College, and I have lived in the Bay Area for my entire life. I am studying Digital Art and Media at San Jose State University, and in my spare time I am learning the fundamentals of UI/UX design through a CalArts course on Coursera. Curently, my artistic style consists of all types of digital art, whether it is illustration, animation, or visual design. I often find inspiration from people, places and things that I love in my life. Much of my work explores themes such as memory and identity, creating profound narratives that evoke a sense of curiosity or introspection. In the future, my goal is to pursue a career in a creative digital environment, such as interactive media, design, illustration, animation, creative art director, or anywhere else where I can flourish and collaborate with others who share the same passion and ideas as me.
